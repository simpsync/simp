Active as a composer and arranger. He is good at producing various music based on the experience of music selection cultivated at a sound effect company. After joining the sound effects company in 2002, he has been involved in the sound effects business for TV programs and DVDs. Since 2006, he has been involved in the stage "Muscle Musical" as a composer. As a member of the project's music production team, he has worked on all stage music and sound effects for about three years, including overseas performances in Las Vegas and elsewhere. To date, he has worked on many stage gekitomo music, produced TV anime gekitomo music, and provided music to commercials and artists. He is capable of consistent work from composition, arrangement, music production, and recording. He owns his own recording studio and is also active as a sound producer.
